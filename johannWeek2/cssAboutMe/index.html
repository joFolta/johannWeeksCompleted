About Me

Johann Folta is a full-stack Javascript web developer at Resilient Coders. He has various experiences in countries throughout the world, such as having embarked on a 4-day motorcycle solo ride through Rwanda and entering North Korea as a curious tourist. Johann strives to make a positive impact in his surrounding communities and previous jobs include working hands-on with stroke patients and the elderly, mentoring adults with developmental disabilities, and providing exceptional customer service as an auto damage appraiser. His interests lie in fast cars, playing soccer, guitar jam sessions, and reptiles. He currently resides in Boston, Massachusetts with his lovely wife Maria and English bulldog named Snooky.
